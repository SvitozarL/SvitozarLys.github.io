Speakers

We are glad to welcome more than twenty speakers, ready to present the latest technologies in the industry. Prepare for an inspirational extravaganza.

Chris Mills

HAVI-METAL CODING

In this unconventional conversation, Chris Mills will encourage you to take the drumsticks in addition to the laptop and beat out the rhythm of learning - both the code and the drums! You will learn the basics of JavaScript and CSS, as well as the drum patterns of heavy metal. This marriage is made in heaven and there is no better antidote to the disappointment of the code than knocking out seven shades of ***** on the drum set.

About the speaker

Chris Mills, a senior technical writer at Mozilla, where he writes documentation and demonstrations about open web applications, Firefox OS and related topics. He likes to work on HTML, CSS, JavaScript and other web technologies and occasionally speaks at conferences and universities.

  • @chrisdavidmills
  • developer.mozilla.org

Shay Hou

Less is better, but better: how constraints cultivate growth

By setting limits, we force ourselves to be more productive. They help us make decisions, focusing on the problem that we are trying to solve. They improve our integrity, which provides the best experience for our users. And they help us grow - a valuable asset in times of innovation.

About the speaker

As a designer and front-end developer, Shay Howe is passionate about solving problems while creating creative and intuitive products. She specializes in the design and development of the interface - specialties, of which he regularly writes and speaks.
